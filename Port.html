Water Level Monitoring (Iot) The IoT-Based Water Level Monitoring System is a smart solution that measures and displays the water level in a tank in real time. It uses an ultrasonic sensor to detect the distance of water from the top of the tank and calculates the water level accordingly. The measured data is shown on an LCD screen, while LED lights indicate different water level conditions (low, medium, and high). This system helps prevent water overflow and dry tank situations, making water management more efficient and automated. 2024
